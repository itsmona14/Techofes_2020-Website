KOLLYWOOD QUIZ

From Ilayaraja to ARR, from Bharathiraja to Atlee put your minds to work at the kollywood quiz at Techofes'2020. Participate in an enthralling quiz that tests your knowledge on the tamil cinema industry.

Round 1:
1.The first round will be a pen and paper round.
2.There will be a maximum of 25 questions.
3.Questions marked with star will decide the winner in case of a tie.
4.The top 8 teams will be selected for next round.
Round 2:
The second round will have 4-6 sub rounds.
1.Bounce and Pounce(Anti-clockwise)
2.Stake Rounds
3.Connections
4.In addition to these, there will be two special rounds. The instructions for each sub round will be briefed in detail during the quiz.
