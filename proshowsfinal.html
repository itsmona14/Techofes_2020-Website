PROSHOWS






CONCERT NIGHT

The much awaited night of Techofes'20, one can feel the vibe of the crowd and see them go crazy. This time with Melody Queen Chinmayi Sripaada, Telugu Playback Singer Vishnupriya Ravi and Music Director cum singer Nivas K Prasanna are going to rock the grand stage.







T-AWARDS

The signature event of techofes for presenting accolades to the best artists in Tamil Cinema Industry.Nearly twenty artists will be honoured on this day followed by mesmerising performances.It is highly popular and the student crowd throngs to have a look at their on-screen role models and more than life heros and heroines.







CHOREO NIGHT

The choreo night boasts of a rich tradition of involvement dance performances by dance groups from different colleges battling it out to be the best.The Talent of Techofes has been a spot of attraction for the students who wish for their voices to be heard.
