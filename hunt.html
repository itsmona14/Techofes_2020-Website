TREASURE HUNT

Rules:
1)Each team can have only a maximum of two members.
2)Participants have to solve the puzzles or riddles and find the correct location.
3)There will be a small parchment pasted in thatlocation consisting of a specific
code or letter.
4)The participants should take a photo of that parchment and report to the
organizers in the registration desk.
5)The next question will be given only after reporting.
6)The first participant to find all the locations is the winner.
7)Please don’t tear off the parchments with codes.

SHERLOCK HUNT

Round 1:
Pen and Paper Round
1)A total of 20 questions will be asked.
2) A team of 2 or 3 members is appreciated.
3) Questions will be mostly of riddles, and some comprise of the series 'Sherlock'
4) Five members will get selected for the second round.

Round 2:
1) A total of 10 questions will be asked
2) Questions mainly comprise of riddles and puzzles
3) Three members will get selected for the third round.

Round 3:
Treasure hunt
1) A single question will be asked
2) Clues will be given. Based on the clues,answer must be found.
3) Those who win in this round will be declared as winner.
